# Proof Checklist — Hormozi ($100M Leads)

Criteria for evaluating case proof strength. Each criterion is a spectrum — left side is stronger.

| # | Stronger | Weaker |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | In-Person | Virtual |
| 2 | Live | Recorded |
| 3 | Raw | Processed |
| 4 | Show | Tell |
| 5 | Other People | You |
| 6 | Identical to Them | Opposite |
| 7 | Personal | Generic |
| 8 | Big Results | Small / None |
| 9 | Newer | Older |
| 10 | More | Less |
| 11 | Third Party Verification | Zero |
| 12 | With Numbers | Without |
| 13 | Metaphors | Jargon |

## Scoring

- Evaluate each criterion (0 or 1, with partial credit for some)
- Final score: 0-10 scale based on criteria met
- Store in the "Nota Prova" column of the Cases spreadsheet

## Improvement Suggestions

After scoring, suggest specific actions to improve weaker criteria. For example:
- Low on "Show" → suggest collecting screenshots or screen recordings
- Low on "Other People" → suggest getting a client testimonial
- Low on "With Numbers" → suggest extracting before/after metrics
